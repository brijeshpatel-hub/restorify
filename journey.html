Restorify — Fueling Growth With What Others Waste

Our Journey

How Restorify began and where we’re headed.

The Problem

With 30–40% of global trash coming from food waste and plastic pollution rising, we saw an urgent need for sustainable, affordable alternatives.

The Spark

We asked: “What if waste could become something valuable?” This led to converting starch‑rich food waste into biodegradable plastic.

Building the Solution

We developed thermoplastic starch (TPS) and a zero‑waste system that produces both bioplastic and nutrient‑rich compost.

The Road Ahead

Our mission is to create affordable, sustainable packaging while making the world cleaner, greener, and more responsible.
